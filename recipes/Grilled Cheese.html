Grilled Cheese

Description

The classic American grilled cheese. Is there anything better on earth than cheese and bread? Possibly the easiest sandwich to make, it hits the spot when craving something salty. Only needing two items, I am sure every child in America has mastered the art of the grilled cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices American Cheese
  • 2 slices White Bread
  • 1 Tablespoon Butter

Steps

  1. Start a small pan over medium low heat and place your butter in
  2. Place your two slices of cheese between your slices of bread
  3. Toast the sandwich in the pan, about 2 minutes on both sides until golden brown. If you are unsure if you have cooked it long enough, lift up one of the slices of bread and check if the cheese is melted
  4. Remove from heat, let stand for about 1 minute, and enjoy!
